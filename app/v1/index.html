How should you judge the clustering?

  • Silhouette: higher means points sit closer to their own cluster than to others.
  • WCSS: lower means clusters are more compact.
  • Adjusted Rand: checks agreement with known labels when labels exist.

A neat PCA picture alone is not enough. Compare the visual pattern with the numeric metrics.
